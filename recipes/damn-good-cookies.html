Damn Good Cookies

Description

Our Damn Good Cookies are a chewy, plant-based treat made with dates, oats, nuts, and dark chocolate chips for natural sweetness and crunch. Packed with fiber, healthy fats, and rich flavor, they’re a wholesome snack that feels indulgent but stays true to clean, nourishing ingredients.

Ingredients

  • ½ cup raw almonds (or raw cashews)
  • ½ cup raw walnuts
  • 6 large pitted dates
  • 1 tbsp vanilla extract
  • 1 tbsp water (only if needed for texture)
  • Pinch of sea salt (optional)
  • 3 tbsp vegan dark chocolate chips
  • 3 tbsp old-fashioned rolled oats
  • Scoop small portions with a spoon, shape into balls or slightly flatten into cookies
  • Serve immediately or refrigerate for a firmer texture.

Steps

  1. In a food processor or blender, pulse the almonds and walnuts until finely ground. Set aside.
  2. In the processor, blend the dates, vanilla extract, and water until a thick paste forms. Scrape down the sides as needed.
  3. Combine the nut flour with the date paste until fully incorporated.
  4. Stir in the oats and dark chocolate chips. Mix gently.

Personal Notes

  • I used cashew instead of almonds. It was perfect!
