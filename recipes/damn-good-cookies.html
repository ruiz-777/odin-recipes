Damn Good Cookies

Description

Our Damn Good Cookies are a chewy, plant-based treat made with dates, oats, nuts, and dark chocolate chips for natural sweetness and crunch. Packed with fiber, healthy fats, and rich flavor, they’re a wholesome snack that feels indulgent but stays true to clean, nourishing ingredients.

Ingredients

  • ½ cup raw almonds (or raw cashews)
  • ½ cup raw walnuts
  • 6 large pitted dates
  • 1 tbsp vanilla extract
  • 1 tbsp water (only if needed for texture)
  • Pinch of sea salt (optional)
  • 3 tbsp vegan dark chocolate chips
  • 3 tbsp old-fashioned rolled oats
